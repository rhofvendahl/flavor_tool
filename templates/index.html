Welcome to the Flavor Tool!

The top left box lists all currently present ingredients. To add an ingredient, just search and click (or press enter); to remove an ingredient press the "X" (or backspace). You can use the "all" and "none" buttons to add or remove in bulk.

The main window displays each present ingredient as a color-coded node:

  • light green=leafy green
  • green=vegetable
  • orange=fruit
  • brown=nut or seed
  • tan=dressing component
  • grey=other

There are three ways to manipulate these ingredients:

  1. Click to select an ingredient, marking it to include in the salad. Click again to de-select.
  2. Double click to lock an ingredient. If you auto-generate a salad, these ingredients will be used as a base. Double click again to unlock.
  3. Right click an ingredient to show which other ingredients pair with it. Black lines are strong pairs, grey lines are weaker.

Selected ingredients that pair well together will be connected with a solid line (black for strong, grey for week), while selected ingredients that clash will be marked with a red line (lighter red for minor clash).

When you've selected a set of ingredients you like, try locking them and pressing "Generate", on the left (or spacebar). This will draw on a bunch of flavor data to select some other ingredients that (hopefully) complement the ones you've locked, filling in the missing pieces.

The "Save" and "Load" buttons let you save multiple projects, so you can play around with different configurations, or pick up where you left off.

Enjoy! :-)
